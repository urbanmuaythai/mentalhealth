THE 3,000
PUSH-UP CHALLENGE
Our member Courtney is taking on 3,000+ push-ups this June for Mental Health Awareness Month — and we're matching every single rep with a donation.
    Why We're Doing This
Mental health affects all of us
Mental health is something so many people quietly struggle with. We know that staying active and improving your fitness can make a real, positive difference to how you feel.
Life is meant to be enjoyed. Everyone goes through hard periods — but if sadness or low mood is sticking around longer than it should, that's not something to push through alone. It's something to talk about.
This challenge is our way of getting the gym moving, starting conversations, and putting our money behind a cause that matters.
    How It Works
10 classes. Last person standing. Every rep counts.
01
 10 selected classes across the week take part in the challenge.
02
 At the end of class, everyone who wants to join in gathers together and starts doing push-ups at the same time.
03
 The group keeps going until people can no longer maintain good form. As members stop, they stand and cheer on those still going.
04
 It's last person standing — the challenge ends when the final remaining participant completes their last push-up.
Every Push-Up Counts
$1 per push-up
Whatever the final count is in each class, that's the donation. All 10 class totals get added together for the overall amount raised.

    If You Need Someone
Suicide is not the answer.
Please reach out.
If you're having thoughts of self-harm or you're in serious emotional distress, please get support straight away. You are not alone, and there are people ready to help right now.
Seeking help early — rather than struggling on your own — is one of the strongest things you can do. Speaking up is always welcomed here.
Talk to Richie — reach out directly any time you need support.
Your GP or healthcare professional — a good first step for ongoing support.
Lifeline — 13 11 14, available 24/7.
Beyond Blue — 1300 22 4636, available 24/7.
