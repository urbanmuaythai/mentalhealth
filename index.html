Why We're Doing This

Mental health
affects all of us

Mental health is something so many people quietly struggle with. We know that staying active and improving your fitness can make a real, positive difference to how you feel.

Life is meant to be enjoyed. Everyone goes through hard periods — but if sadness or low mood is sticking around longer than it should, that's not something to push through alone. It's something to talk about.

This challenge is our way of getting the gym moving, starting conversations, and putting our money behind a cause that matters.

How It Works

Four rounds.
One last person standing.

ROUND01
10 selected classes across the week take part in the challenge.
ROUND02
At the end of class, everyone who wants in gathers together and starts doing push-ups at the same time.
ROUND03
The group keeps going until members can no longer hold good form. As people stop, they stand and cheer on those still going.
FINAL04
It's last person standing — the challenge ends when the final participant completes their last push-up.
Every Push-Up Counts
$1donated per push-up

Whatever the final count is in each class, that's the donation. All 10 class totals get added together for the overall amount raised.

If You Need Someone

Suicide is not
the answer.

If you're having thoughts of self-harm or you're in serious emotional distress, please get support straight away. You are not alone, and help is available right now.

Seeking help early — rather than struggling on your own — is one of the strongest things you can do. Speaking up is always welcomed here.
